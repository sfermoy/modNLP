Upload OMC XML and HED files for indexing

Note: files must be uploaded in in pairs (e.g. omc000001.xml and omc000001.hed), otherwise the checking will fail. You can select several files to upload at once by clicking on 'Browse...' and holding down Ctrl or Shift and clicking on the files you wish to select. Files will be also be checked for character encoding, XML validity, matching filename attribute in the header file, and whether they have already been indexed. The results will appear on the next page, once you hit 'Upload'.
